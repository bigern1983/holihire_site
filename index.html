HOLIHIRE

Established in 2005, based in Protaras and also covering Ayia Napa and surrounding areas, Holihire offers over 15 years of experience, customer service and knowledge in the mobility needs of all generations. We have an extensive range of children's equipement offering everything from stairgates, car/booster seats, high chairs, cots, travel cots and much more.

Our aim is customer satisfaction and to enable you to enjoy your stay in Cyprus by providing the items you need.
